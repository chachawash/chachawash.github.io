What is Qr8?

Qr8  /kjuˋreit/  is your music back office in the cloud. The nine elements below are everything you need to compose the universe of your music business. Stop wondering if you are accidentally step into the future, just get in.

The element starts with “Di” and composes of 12 letters is Distribution, which commands Content Management, Platform Liaison, and Digital Distribution.

The element starts with “Da” and composes of 9 letters is Dashboard, which commands Tracking New Talent and Content, Spotting Trends / Gain Insight, Tracking Sales / Streams, and Audiences Understanding.

The element starts with “Co” and composes of 14 letters is Communications, which commands Public Relations, Corporate Communication, Media Planning, Marketing, and Promotion Strategies.

The element starts with “Ri” and composes of 6 letters is Rights, which commands Copyright Administration, Royalty Collection, and Recording / Publishing Rights Management.

The element starts with “Ac” and composes of 10 letters is Accounting, which commands Bookkeeping, Tax Accounting, Invoicing, and Banking.

The element starts with “Le” and composes of 5 letters is Legal, which commands Music Business Legal Consultation, Intellectual Property Management, Contract Services, and Digital Copyright.

The element starts with “Ar” and composes of 7 letters is Artists, which commands A&R Services, Production Coordination, and Creative Strategy.

The element starts with “Cr” and composes of 10 letters is Creativity, which commands Design and Art / Creative Direction.

The element starts with “Op” and composes of 13 letters is Opportunities, which commands Business Development Opportunities, Corporate Planning, and Business Agent / Negotiation.
