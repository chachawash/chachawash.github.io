Good Design Award.

 

Simple, healthy, convenient and green! The cubic form makes the

unobtrusive but powerful natural detergent powder easier to use, and

optimizes the cost of packaging, transportation and storage. Enjoy the

honestly harmless dish washing experience for body and our planet.

 

Manfred Wang, Jin Kuramoto, Gen Suzuki, Ming-Lung Yu, Wei Hsiung Chan.

Good Design Award Jury.

Good Design Marunouchi.

 

“The fiber of Theaceae plants can be extracted to produce a natural oil. Here, the fiber

have been pulverized and turned into a cubed clump. This makes packaging and transport

easier for the manufacturer and measurement easier for the user. It is made from vegetable-

derived products, so it has very few CO2 emissions, and its non-chemical manufacturing

process is gentle on the environment. This strong design conveys a simple and clear idea.”

 

Wei Hsiung Chan, Aesthetics Commentator.
